The question I am trying to answer with this dashboard is whether there is a link between global temperature rise and natural calamities and also to prove that even in natural disasters, mathematical patterns can be found. I have analyzed data ranging from Volcanoes, Hurricanes, Tsunamis and Landslides. The worst natural disasters are Hurricanes and Earthquakes. Here in this dashboard, I am trying to see if geologists can possibly find a connection between temperature rise during various months and earthquakes.
